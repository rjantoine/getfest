CAUTIONARY NOTE: To ensure maximum enjoyment, members must be fun loving, hard working, creative and enthusiastic. Equipped with original ideas and passionate for a cause. Positive personality is a plus ... Divas/Divos not permitted ;)

Congrats to the Young Ambassadors of GETfest who launched the Cariwest 2k9 Festival entry, for the Movements Dance Company. Their hard work resulted in a first place victory for their Carnival theme “Pirates of the Caribbean”.

Special thanks to our co-chairs Natalie and Richelle for a job well done! Kudos to the group: Brian, Daniel, Lisa, Medgine, Nina, Pheadra, Sarah and Tamecah.

The group also initialized the planning for the upcoming Walk/Run fundraiser that will be held next Spring.

Stay tuned!
