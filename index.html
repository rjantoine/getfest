We get passionate about motivating students to come together and embrace the diversity and multiculturalism of Canada!

We get energized about promoting unity in our communities among the youth…for they are our future leaders!

We get excited about nurturing and inspiring the youth to realize their capacity for growth!

We get fired up about empowering all youth to take action against any form of discrimination and racism!

And, we get elated when we see the youth come together to appreciate the endless possibilities of their true potential!

That is the essence of the Getting Everyone Together youth festival (GETfest)!
